Artist Statement

Cultural expression satiates the human need to create and intellectual desire to understand. People extend their intimate feelings to the world through infinitely diverse means - literature, music, and digital media to name a few. Creating a supportive environment for people of all backgrounds to celebrate each other's art is my driving force. In this web development course, I aim to learn the fundamental skills for creating such a space.
